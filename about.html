Who we are

We are one of the Top Solar energy and sustainable development company in India

Vrita Energy Engineering Private Limited is an innovative and fast-growing startup based in Jharkhand, India, with a steadfast commitment to reshaping the energy sector through renewable energy solutions. Established with the core belief that sustainable practices are essential for a healthier future.

Vrita Energy Engineering is at the forefront of the movement to harness solar energy as a clean, reliable, and sustainable power source. Our mission extends beyond business—it is about transforming the energy landscape in India and contributing to the global effort to combat climate change. We excel in both the service and manufacturing sectors, delivering comprehensive, high-quality solar solutions that are both affordable and accessible, addressing the energy needs of industries, commercial entities, and households.

With a customer-centric approach and a commitment to innovation, we are determined to make clean energy the standard rather than the exception.

Our Vision

At Vrita Energy Engineering, we envision a future where sustainable energy powers the world. Our goal is to foster a clean and green environment, free from the harmful emissions of fossil fuels, and to support the global shift toward renewable energy. We believe that through solar energy, we can drive this change, offering scalable, clean power solutions that will transform industries and communities. We are committed to reducing carbon footprints and environmental degradation by making solar energy the primary source of power across various sectors. Our vision aligns with India's broader goal of becoming a renewable energy leader on the global stage.

Our Mission

At Vrita Energy Engineering, our mission is clear: to empower communities and businesses by delivering dependable, cost-efficient, and sustainable solar energy solutions. We are committed to creating environmentally responsible energy systems that meet the growing demand for clean power. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and adopting best practices in the renewable energy sector, we strive to make clean energy accessible to all, while promoting environmental sustainability and reducing dependence on fossil fuels.
