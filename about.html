Who we are

We are one of the Top Solar energy and sustainable development company in India

Vrita Energy Engineering Private Limited is an innovative and fast-growing startup based in Jharkhand, India, with a steadfast commitment to reshaping the energy sector through renewable energy solutions. Established with the core belief that sustainable practices are essential for a healthier future.

Vrita Energy Engineering is at the forefront of the movement to harness solar energy as a clean, reliable, and sustainable power source. Our mission extends beyond businessâ€”it is about transforming the energy landscape in India and contributing to the global effort to combat climate change. We excel in both the service and manufacturing sectors, delivering comprehensive, high-quality solar solutions that are both affordable and accessible, addressing the energy needs of industries, commercial entities, and households.

With a customer-centric approach and a commitment to innovation, we are determined to make clean energy the standard rather than the exception.

Utility

We build and operate large grid-scale solar, wind, and hybrid projects across India which supplies renewable energy to government utilities, both at state and center. We supply the energy under long term, fixed price contracts for up to 25 years. We developed India's first utility scale solar project in 2009 and today have a total renewable energy portfolio of over 4.3 GWs, of which over 3 GWs is operational.

Energy transition

Vrita Energy Engineering, a B2B arm of Azure Power, helps commercial and industrial customers plan their sustainability roadmap and achieve 100% clean energy transition across their facilities. Our innovative contracts, for both physical and virtual power, and flexible terms and conditions, allow customers to source clean energy from grid connected or open access solar, wind, hybrid renewable energy projects and battery energy storage systems across the country.
