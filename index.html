About Us

Vrita Energy delivers innovative, affordable solar solutions, transforming India's energy landscape with sustainable practices.

Innovating for a Greener Tomorrow with Solar Power

Vrita Energy Engineering Private Limited is an innovative and fast-growing startup based in Jharkhand, India, with a steadfast commitment to reshaping the energy sector through renewable energy solutions. Established with the core belief that sustainable practices are essential for a healthier future, Vrita Energy Engineering is at the forefront of the movement to harness solar energy as a clean, reliable, and sustainable power source.

About us

What We Do

We are one of the Top Solar energy and sustainable development company in India

We build and operate some of the largest grid-scale Solar power projects in the country, and supply the generated renewable power to government utilities, and independent industrial & commercial customers on long term fixed price contracts. The prices in many cases are at or below prevailing alternatives for these customers. We are committed to lead India’s energy transition by providing sustainable energy solutions and achieve net-zero commitments.

Utility

We build and operate large grid-scale solar, wind, and hybrid projects across India which supplies renewable energy to government utilities, both at state and center. We supply the energy under long term, fixed price contracts for up to 25 years. We developed India's first utility scale solar project in 2009 and today have a total renewable energy portfolio of over 4.3 GWs, of which over 3 GWs is operational.

Energy transition

Vrita Energy Engineering, a B2B arm of Azure Power, helps commercial and industrial customers plan their sustainability roadmap and achieve 100% clean energy transition across their facilities. Our innovative contracts, for both physical and virtual power, and flexible terms and conditions, allow customers to source clean energy from grid connected or open access solar, wind, hybrid renewable energy projects and battery energy storage systems across the country.
